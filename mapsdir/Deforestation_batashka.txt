This map was made via the complex analysis of two satellite images of Batashka mountain in the Rhodopes, the borders of which were digitalized from a topographic map. The imagery was taken from Copernicus browser and since there were significant changes in the water bodies reflectivity, extra work was required. With precise calibration of the ISO Cluster unsupervised classification tool and some changes in editor's mode, two correctly classified layers were made. With raster tools the change detection layer was formatted and the legend displays changes the way it is intended in bulgarian landscape studies.
