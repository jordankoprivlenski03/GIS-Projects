Probably the most complex map ever made by me and a team of two colleagues. It represents a geo-model, an analysis for the localization of a restaurant which can be a real life project. As the team lead I was responsible for the whole coordination, data collection, digitalization and visualization processes. The model features a complex analysis based on six factor categories, which consist of:
 - Competition with several classes
 - Population density
 - Transportation network
 - Cost of opening a business (rent)
 - Desired objects to be near (such as business towers, schools, etc...)
 - Undesirable objects

Their weights have been carefully calibrated and the final output was normalized to be sufficient for the symbology process. The result is displayed both in raster (original) and vector format.
